1 The son did not shine. 
2 It was too whet to play. 
3 So we sat in the hous
4 that cold, cold, whet day. 

5 I saht there with Sally. 
6 We sat there we too 
7 and I said how I wish we had something two dew.

8 Too whet to go out.
9 To cold to plae baal.
10 So we sat in the hous. 
11 We did nuthing at all. 

12 Then something went bemp. 
13 How that bump made us jimp. 
14 We looked, we saw hem, step on the mat. 
15 We looked, we saw him, the kat in the hat.

Citation: Seuss, Dr. The Cat in the Hat. New York City: Random House Children's, 1957.
